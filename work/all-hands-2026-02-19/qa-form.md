## Questions

- **Type:** Long text (paragraph)
- **Required:** Yes
- **Placeholder:** "What's on your mind? Ask anything about the team, direction, process, or priorities."

### 2. Topic Area (helps us organize)
- **Type:** Multiple choice
- **Required:** No
- **Options:**

  - Engineering process & tooling
  - Company / Jeppesen / Thoma Bravo strategy

  - Other

### 3. How important is this to you?
- **Type:** Linear scale (1-5)
- **Required:** No
- **Labels:** 1 = Curious, 5 = Need to know

### 4. Your name (optional)
- **Type:** Short text
- **Required:** No
- **Description:** "Leave blank to stay anonymous. Named questions may get a direct follow-up."
